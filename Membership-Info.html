Membership Levels

Emerging:
This level is designed for new and aspiring artists who wish to join our community. Artists become eligible for this membership by having their work successfully selected for inclusion in the annual exhibition, following a formal application process. It provides an excellent opportunity for emerging talent to gain recognition, connect with fellow artists, and begin their journey toward higher membership levels.

Associate/Signature:
These advanced and signature memberships recognize artists who demonstrate a significant commitment to their craft and the organization. Master Pastelist:
The Master Pastelist designation is the pinnacle of achievement within the organization, awarded only to Signature members who have consistently demonstrated extraordinary artistic excellence. To qualify for this prestigious honor, members must accumulate five awards at the annual exhibition, showcasing their exceptional talent and dedication to their craft. Master Pastelist members are regarded as leaders in the artistic community and serve as an inspiration for both emerging and established artists alike.
